James McCaffrey Multi-Swarm Particle Optimization

Visualization of James McCaffrey's Multi-Swarm Optimization. Large circles represent the current best solutions for each swarm. Small circles represent the particles of each swarm by color. The particles and swarm will begin to gain consensus on the location of better and better solutions.

Rastrigan's function has an optimal solution at (0.0,0.0). Mechanisms to avoid local minima include chances to swap particles between swarms and a chance for a particle to die and generate a new random particle.
